Visit Nablus

Enjoy History and Culture in this beautiful city in Palestine.

Top four activities to do at Nablus

Relax at Al-Bazan Resort.

Enjoy a relaxing day at Al-Bazan Resort, where you can swim, sunbathe, and enjoy the beautiful views of the surrounding mountains.

Explore the rich history and visit historical sites.

Visit the old town of Nablus and explore its historical sites and the beautiful ancient architecture, including the famous Jacob's Well and the Al-Nasr Mosque.

Taste the delicious Nablus knafeh.

Don't miss the chance to try the famous Nablus knafeh at AL-Aqsa sweets shop inside of the old town's market.

Visit the beautiful parks and enjoy the nature.

Visit the beautiful parks in Nablus, such as the Al-Nasr Park and the Al-Masri Park, where you can enjoy a peaceful walk and relax in nature.
